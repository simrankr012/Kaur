Modern Life Journey

Posted on April 04, 2017

Lying as a lifeless creature, I turned my face towards the city. There was everything on the streets we could ever wish for, except the peace which we never asked for.

Racing in cars of different milege with people of different ages, all they want is to reach the treasure . They only care about their pace only when they find someone else moving ahead of them.

They ignore the sky which will engulf them one day. They don’t sit on the grounds and talk to the one who created them. He is remembered only when their loved ones are gone .

They don’t notice the way they breathe. How their lungs inflate. How their eyes move , how their eyeballs grow when they look at their lovers.How their feet carry their weight.

All They want is to argue on being theist and atheist rather than accepting what others have to say. One kiss on his cheek and the other moment on lips of different taste. They don’t care about love amyway. They walk that way just because their enemies do. All they want is to snatch their lovers away.

Their own beauty doesn’t amaze them because they are too busy looking on others waists. Abusing while Crossing the beggers , they smile when they put on their best clothes.

Fixing my eyes on the world beneath me. I puff another cigarette And the next moment I say holla to my creator.
